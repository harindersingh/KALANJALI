“Leg it!” :Gully Football

Registration fee: Rs. 150 per team.
Rules:

  • Each team will contain a maximum of 8 players (5 on field + 3 substitutes).
  • Game time: Approx. 15 min (7 min each half).A goalless match will result in a penalty shoot-out with 5 members taking one penalty each (One step penalty from the spot).
  • Only underarm throw allowed to the goal keeper. If not followed, 2 warnings will be given and the third will result in a penalty to the opposite team.
  • Every out results in a kick in.
  • If any player is given a red card, he will not be allowed to continue playing and the team plays with a player short.
  • Failure to report on time results in a walkover to the opposite team.
  • REFEREE’S DESCISION IS FINAL.
